System Overview

UTECH-CARE is your campus medical support system designed to make managing health records, medicine inventory, and scheduled check-ups simple and efficient.

  • Student Log: Record symptoms, visits, and treatments.
  • Medicine List: Track clinic medicine stock and usage.
  • Check-Up Schedule: Manage medical appointments and student check-ups.

About Us

UTechCare is a student-centered clinic management web application designed to make health services more accessible, organized, and efficient within the university. Created as a mini web project, UTechCare streamlines everyday clinic operations to better support both students and clinic personnel.
